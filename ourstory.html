Our Story

SILFEN is an innovative Copenhagen based fashion brand, established in 2013, with a strong focus on sustainability. Behind SILFEN stands the siblings Daniel and Celine Silfen. Both have a deep passion for danish design and growing up, their childhood was filled with beautiful bags. They love to build up the brand’s DNA with a new and playful aesthetic towards the key accessory – the bag. Characteristic of SILFEN is a colorful and playful universe, where innovation and risk taking go hand in hand. We deliver enchanting bags of high quality created in genuine materials with various prints and textures. The bags are negotiated at reasonable prices, which allows you to have a bag for any occasion, whether it’s handbags, tote bags, shoulder bags, fanny bags and the like. As a young, global fashion brand, it is our duty to take care of the planet, not only for our own sake, but for all future generations to come. That’s why every piece is made with sustainable fabrics for an experience that is not only luxurious but responsible. Vegan bags anyone? Our collections are released as limited editions because we are mindful of quality and quantity’s impact on both our present and our future.
