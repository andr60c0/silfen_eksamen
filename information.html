Delivery in Europe: 3-9 business days
Delivery outside of Europe: 8-10 business days

RETURNS

If, for any reason, you are not satisfied with the items purchased, you can return your purchase within 21 days for a full refund. The return period is counted from the day you receive your goods. When returning goods please ensure that you fill in and enclose the return form. The return form will be enclosed in your delivery. If you have not received one, please contact us. All items must be returned unused. All original packaging, price labels etc shall be returned with the product, without having been tampered with. All tags must still be attached to the item. Returning from Denmark, please contact us to receive a prepaid return label (for orders more than 500 DKK) and attach it to your return parcel. If your order total is less than 500 DKK, you are responsible for return yourself. Return transit time in Denmark 1-3 days. Returning from outside of Denmark please find the address for returns on the return form. Return transit time outside of Denmark 7-10 days. Costs incurred in returning are for your own account, and please remember that you as a customer are responsible for the package until it reaches our warehouse. Any and all expenses from tolls, importing etc. is paid for by the costumer. Once the package is received at our warehouse and approved, the purchase price will be refunded to your account.
