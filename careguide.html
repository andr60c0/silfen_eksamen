TREATMENT OF LEATHER

For the care of our leather bags, we recommend Adaxâ€™s leather conditioner and leather protection spray. Adax leather conditioner is made from mineral grease and oil without color, which highlights the color of the leather and prolongs the life of the product. TREATMENT OF NYLON

Nylon is super easy to deal with. Simply clean the bag with a slightly damp cloth.

TREATMENT OF SUEDE

Suede is very delicate, so you have to be very careful about how you impregnate it. Never use leather conditioner or other conditioner on suede. Always impregnate suede bags before use, to make it water, dirt and grease repellent. Use the leather protection spray. For stains, use a suede brush very gently. Always try on a small area first.

HOW TO USE LEATHER CONDITIONER

Nylon is super easy to deal with. Simply clean the bag with a slightly damp cloth.

TREATMENT OF SUEDE

1. Remove dirt with a damp cloth and let it dry
2. Use the corresponding sponge or a colorless and lint-free cloth to apply a thin layer of leather conditioner in a circular manner
3. Let the bag dry before using it again 4. Once the bag has stood for a few minutes, wipe it with a dry cloth to remove any remnants of leather conditioner that the bag has not absorbed
